Nilesh Trivedi

I’m a designer, writer, ethnographer, and educator. A recent Ph.D. grad from Stanford University, I’m currently a visiting professor in Tokyo at Keio University’s Graduate School of Media Design, where I co-direct a joint program with the Royal College of Art, London, and Pratt Institute, New York. I teach my students how to study people, develop empathy, and improve human lives through design and education.

I’m also a Bay Area native, a songwriter, and a film photographer.
